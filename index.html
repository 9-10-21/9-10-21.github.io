Product

prInstant is a non-traditional, modular 3D printer that aims to be more time-efficient and effective than the traditional 3D printers. It uses multiple nozzles and a freely-moving printing head to simultaneously print multiple layers of an object.

Components

The PrInstant comes in 3 parts: the printing head, the connecting tube and the material resevoir. The printing head has multiple nozzles that are activated dependent on if it is viable to use more during a print. In addition, it is made of a very flexible material able to able to simultaneously print on multiple elevations. The connecting tube is made of an elastic material allowing the printer head to move without restrictions. Finally, the material resevoir is made of a sturdy material. All 3 parts are easily connected to one another through a simple plug-and-lock system.

How it works

Rather than stacking multiple 2D layers to create a 3D object, a PrInstant machine prints multiple layers simultaneously. A PrInstant machine starts by creating a core for the object. After, the printing head goes around the core, printing multiple layers at a time. As a result, 3D printing times decrease by 10 fold.
